Angular Lifecycle

ngOnChanges
Called after a bound input property changes
ngOnInit
Called once component is initilized
ngDoCheck
Called during every change detection run
ngAfterContentInit
Called after content (ng-content) has been projected into view
ngAfterContentChecked
Called every time projected content has been checked
ngAfterViewInit
Called after the conponent's view (and child views) has been initilized
ngAfterViewChecked
Called every time view (and child views) has been checked
ngOnDestroy
Called once the component is about to destroyed
